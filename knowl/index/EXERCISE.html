  7. \(\displaystyle y=\cos^4(\theta)-\sin^4(\theta)\)
  8. \(\displaystyle y=\cos(5\theta^3-2\theta^2-3\theta+12)\)
  9. \(\displaystyle y=\sin^2(7\pi \theta) + \cos^2(7\pi \theta)\)
