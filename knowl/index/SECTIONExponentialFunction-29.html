Drill 8.15.

Show that the equation of the line tangent to each function below at the given point is the given line.
  1. Function: \(y= \dfrac{x}{e^x}\text{,}\)
    Point: \((0,0)\)
    Tangent line: \(y = x\)
  2. Function: \(y= \dfrac{x}{e^x}\text{,}\)
    Point: \(\left(1,1/e\right)\)
    Tangent line: \(y = 1/e\)
  3. Function: \(y= \dfrac{e^x}{4x^2+3x-5}\text{,}\)
    Point: \(\left(0,-1/5\right)\)
    Tangent line: \(y = \frac{8}{25}x-\frac{1}{5}\)
  4. Function: \(y= \dfrac{e^x}{4x^2+3x-5}\text{,}\)
    Point: \((1,e/2)\)
    Tangent line: \(y -\dfrac{e}{2}= -\dfrac{9e}{4}(x-1)\)
