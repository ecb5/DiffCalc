Drill 8.18.

For each of the following, assume that \(x=x(t)\text{,}\) \(y=y(t)\text{,}\) and \(z=z(t)\text{.}\) Find an equation relating \(\dfdx{x}{t}\text{,}\) \(\dfdx{y}{t}\text{,}\) and \(\dfdx{z}{t}\text{.}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle e^y=x^2+3z-2\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \sin(xy)=(e^z)^y\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \tan(x+z)=e^ye^x\)
  4. \(\displaystyle e^{xyz}=3\)
  5. \(\displaystyle x^2+y^2+z^2=2\)
  6. \(\displaystyle e^{x^2+y^2+z^2}=2\)
