Drill 8.9.4.

Find approximate solutions for each of the following equations:
  1. \(\displaystyle e^x=5\)
  2. \(\displaystyle e^x=\frac{1}{\sqrt{e}}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle e^{3x}=7\)
  4. \(\displaystyle e^{x/3}=12\)
  5. \(\displaystyle 2^{x}=3^x\)
  6. \(\displaystyle e^{x-5}=e^5\)
  7. \(\displaystyle 7^{x^2-x}=e\)
  8. \(\displaystyle 7e^{x+3}=2\)
  9. \(\displaystyle e^{2x}-3e^x+2=0\)
  10. \(\displaystyle e^{2x}- e^x-2=0\)
  11. \(\displaystyle \sin(e^x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\)
