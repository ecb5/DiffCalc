Drill 6.3.7.

Differentiate both sides of each trigonometric identity to get another identity. Verify each identity (including the one you compute) by graphing the expression on both sides of the equals sign.
  1. \(\displaystyle \cos(2\theta) =\cos^2(\theta)-\sin^2(\theta)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \tan(2\theta)=\frac{2\tan(\theta)}{1-\tan^2(\theta)}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \sec(2\theta)=\frac{1}{\cos^4(\theta)-\sin^4(\theta)}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \cos^2\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)= \frac12(1+\cos(\theta))\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \sin^2\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)=\frac12(1-\cos(\theta))\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \tan\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)=\frac{\sin(\theta)}{1+\cos(\theta)}\)
