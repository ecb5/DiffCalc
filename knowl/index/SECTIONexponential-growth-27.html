Example 8.13.

Find the derivative of \(y=e^{x}\sin(3x).\)
Using the Product Rule, we have
\begin{align*} \dx{\left(e^{x}\sin(3x)\right)}\amp =e^{x}\dx{(\sin(3x))} + \sin(3x)\dx{(e^{x})}\\ \amp =e^{x}\cos(3x)\dx{(3x)} + \sin(3x)e^{x}\dx{x}\\ \amp =e^{x}\cos(3x)3\dx{x} + \sin(3x)e^{x}\dx{x}\\ \dfdx{\left(e^{x}\sin(3x)\right)}{x}\amp =e^{x}\left[3\cos(3x) + \sin(3x)\right] \end{align*}
