If \(x\) is the tangent of \(y\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} x=\tan(y)\text{,} \end{equation*}
then we say that \(y\) is the arctangent of \(x\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} y=\arctan{x}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Speaking loosely, we’d like for the arctangent to be a function which “undoes” the tangent.
