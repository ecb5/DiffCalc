Problem 10.3.2.4.

For each of the following IVPs sketch the slope field in the ranges given and use your slope field to sketch the solution of the IVP.
(a)
\(\dfdx{y}{t}=\sqrt{y},\text{ }y(0)=1,\text{ }-5\le t, \text{ }y\ge0\)
Hint.
It is often simplest to begin by sketching slopes at integer coordinate points in order to get a clearer sense of the graphs. Then to fill in between them.
(b)
\(\dfdx{y}{t}=y^2,\text{ }y(-1)=-2,\text{ }-5\le t\le 5,\text{ }-5\le y\le 5\)
(c)
\(\dfdx{y}{t}=(y-1)^2,\text{ }y(1)=0,\text{ }-5\le t\le 5,\text{ }-5\le y\le 5\)
(d)
\(\dfdx{y}{t}=y(y-2),\text{ }y(1)=1,\text{ }-1\le x\le 1,\text{ }-2\le y\le 3\)
(e)
\(\dfdx{y}{t}=y(2-y),\text{ }y(1)=1,\text{ }-1\le t\le 1,\text{ }-2\le y\le 3\)
(f)
\(\dfdx{y}{t}=(y^2-1),\text{ }y(0)=1,\text{ }-2\le t\le 2,\text{ }-2\le y\le 2\)
