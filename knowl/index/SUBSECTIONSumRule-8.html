Show that \(\dx(x+(y+z))=\dx{x}+\dx{y}+\dx{z}\)

\begin{equation*} \dx(x_1+x_2+x_3+\cdots + x_n)=\dx{x_1}+\dx{x_2}+\dx{x_3}+\cdots + \dx{x_n} \end{equation*}
where \(n\) is an arbitrary positive, integer.
