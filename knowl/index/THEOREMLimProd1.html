Theorem 12.7. The Limit of a Product.
If \(\limit{x}{\pm\infty} {f(x)}=L\) and \(\limit{x}{\pm\infty}{g(x)}=M\) then
\begin{equation*} \limit{x}{\pm\infty} {\left(f(x)\cdot g(x)\right)}=\left(\limit{x}{\pm\infty}{f(x)}\right)\cdot\left(\limit{x}{\pm\infty}{g(x)}\right) = L\cdot M. \end{equation*}
