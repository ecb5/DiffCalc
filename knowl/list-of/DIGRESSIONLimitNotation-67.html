Problem 12.1.1.23.

(a)
\(\limit{x}{\infty}{\frac{x^5+x^3}{5x^5-100x}} =\frac15\)
(b)
\(\limit{x}{-\infty}{\frac{2x^5+x^3}{5x^5-x}} =\frac25\)
(c)
\(\limit{x}{\infty}{\frac{x^5+x^3}{5x^4-10x}} =\infty\)
(d)
\(\limit{x}{-\infty}{\frac{x^5+x^3}{5x^4-10x}} =-\infty\)
(e)
\(\limit{x}{\infty}{\frac{x-\frac{1}{x^3}}{5x-\frac{-8}{x^2}}} =\frac15\)
(f)
\(\limit{x}{-\infty}{\frac{x+\frac{1}{x^3}}{5x+\frac{-8}{x^2}}} =\frac15\)
(g)
\(\limit{x}{\infty}{\frac{x^5+2x^3}{5x^6-x}} =0\)
(h)
\(\limit{x}{-\infty}{\frac{x^5+2x^3}{5x^6-x}} =0\)
