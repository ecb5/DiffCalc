Problem 5.9.1.

(a)

For the Folium of Descartes: \(x^3+y^3=3xy\)
  1. Show that \(\dfdx{y}{x}=\frac{y-x^2}{y^2-x}\text{.}\)
  2. Locate all of the points \((x,y)\neq (0,0)\) where the line tangent to the Folium of Descartes is horizontal.
  3. Locate all of the points \((x,y)\neq (0,0)\) where the line tangent to the Folium of Descartes is vertical.

(b)

The Tschirnhausen cubic: \(27{y}^{2}=\left(1-x\right){\left(8+x\right)}^{2}\)
  1. Show that \(\dfdx{y}{x} = \frac{-(8+x)(3x+6)}{54y}.\)
  2. Locate all of the points \((x,y)\neq (-8,0)\) where the line tangent to the Tschirnhausen cubic is horizontal.
  3. Locate all of the points \((x,y)\neq (-8,0)\) where the line tangent to the Tschirnhausen cubic is vertical.
