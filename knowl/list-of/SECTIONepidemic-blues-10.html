Problem 11.2.3.

(a)
Explain how we know that \(\dfdx{R}{t}\) must be positive.
(b)
Show that
\begin{equation*} \dfdx{I}{t}=(aS-b)I. \end{equation*}
(c)
Use the information in part (b) to show that the number of infected is increasing when \(S\gt \frac{b}{a}\) and decreasing when \(S\lt \frac{b}{a}.\)
(d)
Show that
\begin{equation*} \dfdxn{I}{t}{2}=a^2I\left[S^2-\left(\frac{2b}{a}+I\right)S+\left(\frac{b}{a}\right)^2\right] \end{equation*}
and use this to show that \(\dfdxn{I}{t}{2}\lt 0\) when
\begin{equation*} \frac{b}{a}+\left(\frac{I}{2}-\sqrt{\left(\frac{I}{2}\right)^2+\frac{bI}{a}}\right)\lt S\lt \frac{b}{a}+\left(\frac{I}{2}+\sqrt{\left(\frac{I}{2}\right)^2+\frac{bI}{a}}\right) \end{equation*}
and is positive otherwise.
