Problem 4.3.8.

Compute \(\dx{y}\) for each of the following:
  1. \(\displaystyle y-x=0\)
  2. \(\displaystyle y-x=5\)
  3. \(\displaystyle y-2x=7\)
  4. \(\displaystyle 20x-y=-1\)
  5. \(\displaystyle y=0.0002x\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \frac{y}{x}=\pi\)
  7. \(\displaystyle y=3+2x\)
  8. \(\displaystyle \frac{7-\pi y}{x}= 17\)
  9. \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{2}x\)
  10. \(\displaystyle y=(\pi+\sqrt{2})x\)
  11. \(\displaystyle y=\pi x+\sqrt{2}x\)
  12. \(\displaystyle y=\dfrac{(\pi+\sqrt{2})x}{x}\)
  13. \(\displaystyle y=\dfrac{(1-x)x^4}{x^3-x^4}\)
