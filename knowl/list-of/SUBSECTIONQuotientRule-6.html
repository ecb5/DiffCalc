Problem 4.3.50.

Derive the Quotient Rule, \(\dx{z}=\dfrac{y\dx{x}-x\dx{y}}{y^2}\text{,}\) in two ways:
(a)
By writing \(\dx\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)=\dx(x\inverse{y})\) and applying the Product Rule and the Power Rule.
(b)
By making the substitution \(z=\frac{x}{y}\text{,}\) so that \(yz=x\text{.}\) Apply the Product Rule and solve for \(\dx{z}\text{.}\)
