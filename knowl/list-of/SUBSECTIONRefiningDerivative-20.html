Problem 17.2.3.7.

For each of the following show that \(\tlimit{x}{\infty}{f(x)}=0\text{.}\)
(a)
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{x+2}\)
(b)
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{x^2}\)
(c)
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{x^3}\)
(d)
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{x^3+2}\)
(e)
\(f(x)=-\frac{2}{x}\)
(f)
\(f(x)= \frac{2}{x^2}\)
(g)
\(f(x)= \frac{1}{5x-7}\)
(h)
\(f(x)= \frac{\sin(x)}{x}\)
Hint.
\(\abs{\sin(x)}\le1\text{.}\)}
