Problem 4.35.
(a)
Mimic the process in Examples Example 4.33 and Example 4.34 to show that \(\dx\left(\sqrt[3]{x^4}\right)=\frac43x^{\frac13}\dx{x}.\)
(b)
Now suppose \(y=x^{\frac{p}{q}},\) where \(p\) and \(q\) are positive integers and show that \(\dx\left( x^{\frac{p}{q}}\right)=\frac{p}{q} x^{\frac{p}{q}-1} \dx x.\)
